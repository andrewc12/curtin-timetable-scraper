Timetable for 2020, Semester 1 - As at 14 November 2019 01:06 PM

Please note: Plan Your Timetable is a planning tool only. You must register in your classes using eStudent. Find out how to register for your classes.

How to use this page:
  • The timetable below shows all of the activities (lectures, tutorials etc) available for the units you selected on the previous screen. It shows all of the options that you have to choose from.
  • To see how your timetable would look if you selected a particular activity, scroll past the timetable and click the checkbox for that activity. Then click Update timetable.
  • The timetable will refresh and show only the activities you have selected. You can check if an activity is full by hovering over the activity in the timetable.

If a unit is not shown below, it may not be timetabled. Please contact Curtin Connect and select the 'Class registration' category under 'Managing your course'.
